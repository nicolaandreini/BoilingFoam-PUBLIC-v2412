# boilingFoam-PUBLIC-v2412

Fork of [boilingFoam-PUBLIC](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.7991049)  extended with:

- Turbulent contribution in the energy equation
- Compatibility with **OpenFOAM v2412**
- Yokoi contact angle models
- New tutorial case: `dropletSplash_Yokoi2009`

- F. Municchi, C.N. Markides, O.K. Matar, M. Magnini, *Computational study of bubble, thin-film dynamics and heat transfer during flow boiling in non-circular microchannels*, Appl. Therm. Eng., 238, 122039, 2024. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.applthermaleng.2023.122039
